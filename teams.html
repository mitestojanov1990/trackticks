Performance data and development

How to evaluate the athletic performance of your team? TRACKTICS enables you to gather objective data on the athletic performance of each of your players. You can monitor the development throughout each single session and over time.

Motivate

Supporting your subjectivity with objective performance data will facilitate your coaching and allow to give profound, individual data based feedback.

This will assist you motivating and inspiring your players to unleash their potential.

The balance between load and recovery

Comprehensive visualisations of load parameters, like distance of number of accelerations & decelerations, after every session will help you to balance load and recovery on a players level. During the recovery period of a player, you can monitor his athletic performance KPIs and compare with pre-injury data.

Improved planning & decision making

Choose consciously the types of training based on objective performance data. High and low performers to be quickly identified and addressed. Support your decisions with objective data. Use the insights you gain from the TRACKTICS data when making tough decisions as a coach.

Comparison - putting your
data into perspective

Compare your players and identify individual strengths and weaknesses. The TRACKTICS app will highlight who outperformed or underperformed when it really mattered. The possibility of comparing your players with external benchmarks will add another perpective when analysing their performance.
